## Why puzzle games are good for you

There's real appeal beyond fun: puzzle games ask you to plan, spot patterns and think a move ahead, all in short, low-pressure bursts. They're the perfect "brain snack" for a coffee break — engaging enough to feel worthwhile, short enough not to swallow your afternoon. And because they run in the browser, you can play a level on your phone, then pick up on your laptop later.
